Pizza Doce

Ingredients for Dough

  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of fresh yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 8 cups of flour
  • Water

Pizza Toppings

  • 3 cups of semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 6 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • Chocolate sprinkles (optional)
  • Strawberries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Dilute the yeast in the sugar and add the eggs, salt, butter and flour. Gradually add water, while kneading, until it unglues from your hands. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Divide the dough into 8 portions and roll them into the desired shape with the rolling pin. Arrange on baking sheets and bake in a preheated medium oven (400F) for about 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
  3. For brigadeiro, melt the chocolate with the butter over low heat, stirring constantly. Add the condensed milk and keep stirring until it thickens.
  4. Cover the pizzas with the brigadeiro (take it back to the oven if you need to heat it up) and put strawberries and sprinkles on top.
